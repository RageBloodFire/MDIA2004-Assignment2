What is COVID-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.
It can cuase serious respiratory complications that may result in death.
Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.
Those who have underlying respiratory illnesses are more at risk for a fatal contraction of the virus.

WASH HANDS

Wash your hands regularly to kill germs and bacteria.

DISTANCING

Stay at least 7 feet away from each other.

STAY HEALTHY

Eat well. Exercise. Take vitamins. Rest properly.

Jamaica vs COVID-19

Currently, Jamaica has over 500 confirmed cases of COVID-19, of which over 300 are females. Approximately 211 of the reported positive cases have already recovered, while 9 of them have resulted in death.

Over the past 3 months, Prime Minister Hon. Andrew Holdness and the ministry of health have implemented many regulations in an attempt to reduce the spread of the virus. One such ruling is the all-island curfew.

The government has now decided that they should reopen the borders. Prime Minister, the Most Hon. Andrew Holness, is appealing for Jamaicans repatriated under the controlled re-entry protocols to follow the rules outlined by the Government to contain the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19).
